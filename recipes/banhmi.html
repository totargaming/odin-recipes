Banh Mi

Vietnam's underdog

Instruction

Banh Mi is a Vietnamese sandwich that combines French and Vietnamese culinary influences. Follow these steps to make your own delicious Banh Mi:

Ingredients

  1. Baguette or Vietnamese bread roll
  2. Grilled or roasted pork, thinly sliced
  3. Carrots, julienned
  4. Daikon radish, julienned
  5. Fresh cilantro

Preparing

20 minutes

Processing

15 minutes

Serving

5 minutes
