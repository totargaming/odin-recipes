Pho

Vietnam's pride

Instruction

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, and meat, usually beef or chicken. Follow these steps to make delicious Pho at home:

Ingredients

  1. 1 lb beef or chicken, thinly sliced
  2. 8 cups beef or chicken broth
  3. 1 onion, sliced
  4. 3 star anise
  5. 1 cinnamon stick

Preparing

30 minutes

Processing

30 minutes

Serving

20 minutes
