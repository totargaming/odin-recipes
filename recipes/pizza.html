Pizza

Italy's Classic

Instruction

Indulge in the savory delight of homemade pizza. Follow these steps to create your perfect pizza:

Ingredients

  1. 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  2. 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F/43°C)
  3. 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  4. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  5. 1 teaspoon sugar
  6. 1 teaspoon salt
  7. 1 cup pizza sauce
  8. 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  9. Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Preparing

15 minutes

Rising

1 hour

Baking

15 minutes
